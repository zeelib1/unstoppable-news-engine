## üí° Validation Tips

**Customer Interviews:**
- ‚úÖ Ask about pain, not your solution
- ‚úÖ Listen 80%, talk 20%
- ‚úÖ Record with permission
- ‚úÖ Get exact quotes for landing page
- ‚úÖ Ask "would you pay?" explicitly

**Landing Page:**
- ‚úÖ Focus on problem, not features
- ‚úÖ Show pricing (builds trust)
- ‚úÖ Use customer quotes
- ‚úÖ Clear CTA (email capture)
- ‚úÖ Launch special (creates urgency)

**Community Posting:**
- ‚úÖ Provide value, don't just promote
- ‚úÖ Engage with every comment
- ‚úÖ Share learnings, not just links
- ‚úÖ Build relationships long-term
- ‚úÖ Be authentic and vulnerable
