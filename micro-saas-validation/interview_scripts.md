# Customer Interview Scripts

**5 variations for different audiences**  
**Duration:** 15 minutes each  
**Goal:** Understand pain points and validate willingness to pay

---

## Script 1: Content Creators / Indie Hackers

**Opening (1 min)**
```
Hi [Name], thanks for taking time to chat!

I'm researching how people manage their social media presence, and I'd love to learn about your workflow. No sales pitch, just genuinely curious about your process.

Sound good?
```

**Discovery Questions (10 min)**

1. **Current workflow:**
   "Walk me through how you currently share content across social media. Like if you write a blog post, what's your process for promoting it?"

2. **Time investment:**
   "How much time would you say you spend on social media posting per week? Just the posting itself, not creating content."

3. **Pain points:**
   "What's the most annoying part of that process?"

4. **Platforms:**
   "Which platforms do you post to? (Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.)"
   "Are there any new platforms you want to be on but haven't had time? (Bluesky, Threads, Mastodon?)"

5. **Current tools:**
   "Do you use any tools to help with this? Buffer, Hootsuite, etc.?"
   "What do you like/dislike about them?"

6. **Ideal solution:**
   "If you could wave a magic wand, what would make this easier?"

7. **Cross-posting:**
   "Do you post the same content to multiple platforms, or customize for each?"
   "If you customize, what's different? (Character limits, hashtags, etc.)"

**Validation Questions (3 min)**

8. **Problem severity:**
   "On a scale of 1-10, how painful is this problem for you?
   1 = minor annoyance, 10 = losing sleep over it"

9. **Current spend:**
   "Are you currently paying for any social media tools? How much per month?"

10. **Willingness to pay:**
    "If there was a tool that could post to Twitter, LinkedIn, Bluesky, Threads, and Mastodon with ONE click, and handled character limits automatically, what would that be worth to you per month?"
    
    (If they hesitate: "Just ballpark - $10? $30? $50? $100?")

11. **Decision trigger:**
    "What would make you switch from your current setup to something new?"

12. **Urgency:**
    "If I built this, how soon would you want to start using it?
    - This week
    - This month
    - When I have time (low priority)
    - Not sure"

**Closing (1 min)**

13. **Waitlist:**
    "I'm considering building this. If I did, would you be interested in early access at 50% off the regular price?"
    
    (If yes: "Great! Can I grab your email to keep you posted?")

14. **Referrals:**
    "Do you know anyone else who deals with this same pain? Could you intro me?"

**End:**
"This was super helpful, thank you! I'll keep you posted as I make progress."

## Script 2: Small Business Owners / Freelancers

**Opening (1 min)**
```
Hi [Name]! Thanks for chatting with me.

I'm doing research on how small business owners handle their social media marketing. I'd love to hear about what you're doing and what's working (or not working).

No pitch, just curious! Sound good?
```

**Discovery Questions (10 min)**

1. **Social media role:**
   "How important is social media for your business? Lead generation, brand awareness, both?"

2. **Current process:**
   "Who handles social media posting? You, an assistant, an agency?"

3. **Time & frequency:**
   "How often do you post? Daily, few times a week?"
   "How long does it take to post something to all your platforms?"

4. **Platforms:**
   "Which social platforms are you active on?"
   "Are there platforms you wish you were on but don't have bandwidth for?"

5. **Content creation:**
   "Do you create content yourself or repurpose from somewhere? (Blog, newsletter, etc.)"

6. **Biggest challenge:**
   "What's the biggest pain point with social media for your business right now?"

7. **Tools:**
   "Are you using any scheduling tools? What do you like/not like?"

**Validation Questions (3 min)**

8. **Problem priority:**
   "Compared to other business challenges, how high is 'social media management' on your priority list?"

9. **Budget:**
   "Do you have a budget for social media tools/services? What's reasonable per month?"

10. **ROI expectation:**
    "If a tool could save you 5 hours per week on social media posting, what would that be worth?"

11. **Feature importance:**
    "Rank these by importance:
    - Cross-posting to multiple platforms
    - Scheduling posts in advance
    - Analytics/engagement tracking
    - Team collaboration
    - Content suggestions"

12. **Buying authority:**
    "Do you make the decision on tools, or does someone else approve?"

**Closing (1 min)**

13. **Interest:**
    "If there was a simple tool that let you post once and it goes to all your platforms automatically, would you use it?"

14. **Waitlist:**
    "I'm thinking about building this. Want early access at a discount?"

## Script 3: Social Media Managers / Agencies

**Opening (1 min)**
```
Hey [Name], thanks for making time!

I'm researching social media workflow tools, and I wanted to pick your brain since you do this professionally. I'd love to understand your tech stack and what works well or doesn't.

Just learning, no sales pitch! That cool?
```

**Discovery Questions (10 min)**

1. **Client base:**
   "How many clients do you manage social media for?"
   "What types of businesses? (B2B, B2C, specific industries)"

2. **Workflow:**
   "Walk me through your workflow for publishing a post across multiple client accounts and platforms."

3. **Tools:**
   "What tools are you currently using? (Hootsuite, Buffer, Sprout Social, etc.)"
   "What's your total monthly spend on tools?"

4. **Pain points:**
   "What's the most frustrating part of your current tool?"
   "If you could fix one thing, what would it be?"

5. **Platforms:**
   "Which platforms do your clients care most about?"
   "Are you seeing demand for newer platforms? (Bluesky, Threads, Mastodon)"

6. **Team collaboration:**
   "How many people on your team use the tools?"
   "How do you handle approvals, content creation, etc.?"

**Validation Questions (3 min)**

7. **Feature gaps:**
   "What features do you wish your current tool had?"

8. **Platform coverage:**
   "If a new tool supported Twitter, LinkedIn, Bluesky, Threads, Mastodon, and Facebook but was missing Instagram, would that be a dealbreaker?"

9. **Pricing sensitivity:**
   "What do you currently pay per month for social media tools?"
   "What would be too expensive? What would be suspiciously cheap?"

10. **Switching cost:**
    "How hard would it be to switch to a new tool? What would make it worth the hassle?"

11. **Decision factors:**
    "What would make you try a new social media tool?
    - Better price
    - Better features
    - Better UX
    - Better support"

**Closing (1 min)**

12. **Beta interest:**
    "I'm considering building a tool focused on the newer platforms (Bluesky, Threads, Mastodon) that the big players don't support well yet. Would you be interested in trying an early version?"

13. **Feedback partnership:**
    "Would you be open to giving feedback during development? I'd give you lifetime discount in exchange."

## Script 4: Personal Brands / Coaches / Consultants

**Opening (1 min)**
```
Hi [Name]! Appreciate you chatting with me.

I'm talking to people building personal brands to understand how you manage your online presence. I'm just learning, not selling anything!

Mind if I ask a few questions about your social media workflow?
```

**Discovery Questions (10 min)**

1. **Brand goals:**
   "What's your main goal with social media? (Leads, credibility, audience building)"

2. **Posting frequency:**
   "How often do you try to post?"
   "How often do you actually post? (Be honest!)"

3. **Content types:**
   "What types of content do you share? (Thoughts, articles, videos, etc.)"

4. **Cross-posting:**
   "Do you post the same thing to multiple platforms or customize for each?"
   "If you customize, what's different?"

5. **Time commitment:**
   "How much time per day/week goes into social media posting?"

6. **Automation:**
   "Do you use any tools to automate or schedule posts?"
   "What's working? What's not?"

7. **Biggest hurdle:**
   "What stops you from being more active on social media?"

**Validation Questions (3 min)**

8. **Pain scale:**
   "How much does social media management feel like a chore? (1-10)"

9. **ROI awareness:**
   "Do you track which platforms drive the most value for you? (Leads, engagement, etc.)"

10. **Budget:**
    "Are you currently spending money on social media tools or services?"
    "What would be a reasonable monthly cost for a tool that saves you 5 hours/week?"

11. **Feature priority:**
    "Which matters more:
    - Posting to many platforms easily
    - Scheduling content in advance
    - Analytics on what's working
    - Suggestions on what to post"

12. **Platform gaps:**
    "Are there platforms you know you should be on but aren't? Why?"

**Closing (1 min)**

13. **Solution interest:**
    "If there was a dead-simple tool where you write once and it posts everywhere, would you use it?"

14. **Early access:**
    "Want to be on the list for early access if I build this?"

## Script 5: First-Time Founders / Solo Makers

**Opening (1 min)**
```
Hey [Name]! Thanks for hopping on.

I'm talking to founders about how you handle social media for your startup/project. I'm in the same boat and trying to figure out what works.

Mind sharing your approach?
```

**Discovery Questions (10 min)**

1. **Current stage:**
   "What stage is your project at? (Idea, building, launched, growing)"

2. **Social presence:**
   "Which platforms are you posting on?"
   "How active are you? (Daily, weekly, sporadically, not at all)"

3. **Time constraints:**
   "You're probably wearing 10 hats. How much time can you realistically spend on social media?"

4. **Content source:**
   "Where does your content come from? (Product updates, blog posts, thoughts, etc.)"

5. **Manual process:**
   "Right now, how do you post? (Log into each platform manually? Copy-paste?)"

6. **Cross-posting:**
   "Do you post the same thing everywhere or tailor it?"

7. **Pain points:**
   "What's the most annoying part of managing social media as a solo founder?"

**Validation Questions (3 min)**

8. **Build vs Buy:**
   "Would you rather spend time building features or managing social media?"

9. **Budget reality:**
   "What's your monthly budget for tools? (Total, including hosting, etc.)"
   "How much would you pay for a tool that cuts social media time in half?"

10. **Priority:**
    "On a scale of 1-10, how important is social media for your business right now?"

11. **Ideal solution:**
    "If you could build the perfect social media tool for founders, what would it do?"

12. **Audience question:**
    "Do you know other founders with this same problem?"

**Closing (1 min)**

13. **Community:**
    "If I built this, would you help spread the word in founder communities?"

14. **Waitlist:**
    "Want early access at 50% off?"

## Interview Tips

**Before the call:**
- [ ] Review their social media profiles
- [ ] Note which platforms they use
- [ ] Check if they've mentioned social media pain points
- [ ] Prepare personalized opener

**During the call:**
- [ ] Record (with permission) or take detailed notes
- [ ] Listen more than you talk (80/20 rule)
- [ ] Ask "why" to dig deeper
- [ ] Note exact quotes (use for landing page copy)
- [ ] Watch for energy/emotion (signals real pain)

**After the call:**
- [ ] Send thank you email within 24h
- [ ] Document findings in tracking sheet
- [ ] Note: Would pay? How much? Urgency?
- [ ] Add to waitlist if interested
