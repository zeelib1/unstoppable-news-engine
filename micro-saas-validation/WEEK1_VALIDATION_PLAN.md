## üí° Tips for Success

**Customer Interviews:**
- Be genuinely curious, not salesy
- Ask "why" 3 times to dig deeper
- Listen more than you talk
- Record conversations (with permission)
- Focus on pain, not your solution

**Landing Page:**
- Focus on the problem, not features
- Use customer quotes from interviews
- Clear call-to-action (email capture)
- Show pricing (builds trust)
- Launch special (50% off creates urgency)

**Community Engagement:**
- Be helpful, not promotional
- Share learnings, not just your product
- Ask questions, gather insights
- Build relationships (long-term thinking)

**Tracking:**
- Document everything (you'll forget)
- Look for patterns in interviews
- Note exact quotes for copy
- Track where signups come from

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Building before validating** ‚ùå
   - You're doing this right by validating first!

2. **Asking "would you use this?"** ‚ùå
   - Everyone says yes. Ask "would you pay?"

3. **Talking to friends/family** ‚ùå
   - They lie to be nice. Talk to strangers.

4. **Ignoring weak signals** ‚ùå
   - If <10 signups, don't force it. Pivot.

5. **Skipping interviews** ‚ùå
   - Landing page signups ‚â† revenue
   - Conversations reveal truth

6. **Not documenting** ‚ùå
   - Take notes. You'll need quotes for copy.
